Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about starting your India expansion journey

How long does it take to set up a company in India?

+

Typically, we can complete the incorporation of a Private Limited Company within 15-30 days, including all regulatory registrations and bank account setup. The timeline depends on document readiness and specific requirements.

What are the minimum capital requirements?

+

For a Private Limited Company, there's no minimum paid-up capital requirement. However, we recommend a minimum of ‚Çπ1 lakh (approximately $1,200) for operational purposes and credibility.

Do I need to visit India personally for incorporation?

+

No, physical presence is not required for most incorporation processes. We can handle everything remotely with properly notarized documents. However, directors may need to visit for bank account opening in some cases.

What ongoing compliance is required?

+

Indian companies must file annual returns, conduct board meetings, maintain statutory registers, file GST returns (if applicable), and comply with various regulatory requirements. Our Virtual CFO service handles all these seamlessly.

Can foreign nationals be directors of Indian companies?

+

Yes, foreign nationals can be directors of Indian companies. However, at least one director must be a resident of India. We can provide nominee director services if needed.

How do you ensure data security and confidentiality?

+

We maintain strict confidentiality with signed NDAs, use secure document management systems, and follow international data protection standards. Your business information is completely secure with us.
